Our History

We are I-CUBE West Africa Limited, a leading solutions provider in Security Systems Infrastructure, Traffic and Toll Management Solutions with our head office in Lagos, Nigeria. Incorporated on November 28, 2007 under the companies and allied matters act 1990 by the Corporate Affairs Commission of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, the business is poised to redefine technology for infrastructural growth and development in the sub-Saharan Africa region.


I-CUBE W.A. is Integrated Intelligent Imaging West Africa Limited. Because we see a world where local expertise will be key to providing technological solutions, we have set out to become a reference point for effective and efficient service provision whilst generating appropriate returns for our clients, partners and other stakeholders.


As a leader in the provision of security solutions, and Access Control Systems the company has acquired partnership agreements with key OEMs worldwide, this makes us a strong contender in the field of Security, Toll and Car Park Management Systems with an enviable track record for surpassing expectations in this regards. Our service offerings consist of intelligent application of technology for superior engineering backed by an efficient customer support team.

Our Mindset

We have a clearly defined business strategy aimed at developing our market position in our core areas of expertise so that we are recognised as the pioneer and a company of choice for cutting-edge solutions.

Having accomplished significant level of success in the area of international business collaborations, Integrated Intelligent Imaging W/A Ltd is rapidly growing its network of operations and services in order to continue to make strategic progress in new business territories. We pride ourselves in creating and delivering bespoke solutions that not just work, but solve real problems now.

 

Because we want to be known for our service delivery to national problems posing as global challenges for sustainability of development in road infrastructure and public safety, delivering an enviable reputation for tailoring solution to meet the specific needs of our clients is our primary objective. This is why we work with strong international and local alliances that can and will provide a broad range of technical support for the various solutions we offer.

 

In this way we are optimistic of seamless service by ensuring the entire process from initial project feasibility to project completion surpasses the expectation of our clients.
